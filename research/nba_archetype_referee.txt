================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================

1. STRONGEST SIGNAL: crew_high_count (number of high-scoring refs)
   - Monotonic gradient from -1.55 (0 high) to +9.82 (3 high)
   - crew_high=2: ME=+5.35, 60.1%, p<0.001, 3/3 seasons
   - crew_high=0: ME=-1.55, 47.4%, p=0.003, 3/3 seasons
   - This is the cleanest, most stable gradient in all NBA archetype tests

2. Signal direction: BIDIRECTIONAL
   - High-scoring crews → OVER (+5.35 at 2+ high refs)
   - Low-scoring crews → UNDER (-2.68 at 2+ low refs)
   - Market does not adjust for referee crew identity

3. Season stability: 3/3 for both crew_high=2 and crew_high=0
   - crew_high=2 range: +4.70 to +5.81 (extremely tight)
   - crew_high=0 range: -1.30 to -1.81 (tight)

4. Pace control: SURVIVES (adj_ME=+1.93 of +2.46 raw)
   - 78% of signal retained after controlling for team matchups

5. Individual ref outliers confirm:
   - CJ Washington, Jason Goldenberg, Tony Brothers: +4.9 to +5.6 ME
   - Brandon Adair: -5.16 ME (strongest UNDER ref)

6. Independence from Venue: PARTIAL
   - crew_avg_pts Q4 loses significance without Venue games (p=0.115)
   - BUT crew_high_count=2 is computed independently of Venue teams
   - Need to test crew_high=2 after removing Venue overlap

7. Coverage: 67.3% — 33% of games cannot be classified
   - Uniform across seasons (no bias)
   - Live deployment requires reliable pre-game ref assignments

8. Data gaps:
   - No per-game FTA to confirm foul mechanism
   - No OT flag to check overtime inflation
   - No ref pair test possible (no pair hits N>=30)

VERDICT: CONFIRMED SIGNAL — strongest NBA archetype finding

crew_high=2 (two high-scoring refs assigned) is the strongest
independent signal found across all NBA archetype tests:
  ME=+5.35, hit=60.1%, p<0.001, 3/3 seasons consistent
  Survives pace control (+78% retained)
  Monotonic gradient (0→1→2→3 maps cleanly to ME)

DEPLOYMENT CONSIDERATIONS:
  - Pre-game ref assignments typically released 1-2 hours before tip
  - Late timing limits but doesn't prevent use
  - Requires referee classification table maintained in code
  - Need to verify independence from Venue at the game level
  - Need OT check before finalizing sizing
  - Suggested tier: TIER_1B equivalent (1.5u) for crew_high >= 2
  - Suggested tier: CONTEXT for crew_high = 0 (UNDER lean, no bet)
