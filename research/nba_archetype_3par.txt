VERDICT: NULL

The high-3PAr pairing hypothesis fails on multiple dimensions:
  1. Q4 p=0.298 — not significant
  2. Q3 outperforms Q4 — no monotonic pattern
  3. Variance is NOT elevated in Q4 vs Q1 (17.3 vs 17.5)
  4. Right-tail improvement is marginal (+3.2pp at +10 threshold)
  5. Non-Venue Q4 ME = -0.17 — zero independent effect
  6. Season confound present (64.8% of Q4 from one season)
  7. Both-teams-LOW actually outperforms both-teams-HIGH

The Type E mechanism (fat right tail from 3-point variance) is
theoretically plausible but empirically absent. The market
correctly prices 3-point attempt rate into game totals.

Do not deploy as a signal or modifier.
