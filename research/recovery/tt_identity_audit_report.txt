FINDING 1 -- THREE DIFFERENT OBJECTS:
  A. Phase 6 report (the "58%%/56%%" claims):
     - Formula: HOME_SHARE=0.5015, TRUNCATION=0.248, SP=xFIP, baseline=4.231
     - Used feature_table xFIP (potentially look-ahead)
     - Hit rates reported: 56-58%% at various thresholds
     - Against: posted TT lines from team_totals_historical.parquet

  B. Backtest v1/v2 scripts (research/team_totals/):
     - Completely different methodology: Monte Carlo simulation
     - Uses S2 starter-path model + negative binomial draws
     - Identifies "suppressed team" by sim-derived expected runs
     - Bets suppressed team TT under when run_gap >= threshold
     - This is NOT the same formula as Phase 6 or live

  C. Live signal (mlb/pipeline/team_total_signal.py):
     - Formula: HOME_SHARE=0.5015, TRUNCATION=0.248, SP=ERA, baseline=4.50
     - Uses pitcher_game_logs expanding ERA (PIT-safe, shift(1))
     - Gets starter identity from MLB Stats API probablePitcher
     - No prices logged
     - Degraded mode: falls back to league avg ERA when pitcher unknown

FINDING 2 -- FORMULA DRIFT (Phase 6 Report -> Live):
  - SP metric changed: xFIP -> ERA (different stat entirely)
  - League baseline changed: 4.231 -> 4.50
  - These changes were NOT validated with a backtest
  - The 58%%/56%% claims from Phase 6 report CANNOT be attributed to
    the live formula because the live formula uses different inputs

FINDING 3 -- POTENTIAL LOOK-AHEAD in Phase 6:
  - Feature table xFIP may be end-of-season (check std analysis above)
  - If look-ahead, the 58%%/56%% claims are inflated

FINDING 4 -- PRICE GAP:
  - All backtests used flat -110 ROI
  - Live signal logs NO prices
  - Real TT under prices may carry heavier juice
  - At true vig, break-even is higher than 52.4%%

VERDICT: NEVER-MATCHED
======================================================================

The live TT signal was NEVER the same object as the original backtest.
Three distinct methodologies exist:
  1. Phase 6 formula (xFIP-based, possibly look-ahead)
  2. Backtest v1/v2 (simulation-based suppressed team)
  3. Live signal (ERA-based formula, different baseline)

The 58%%/56%% hit rates from Phase 6 were generated with xFIP (baseline
4.231). The live signal uses ERA (baseline 4.50). These are different
stats with different distributions. No backtest validated the ERA switch.

Additionally, the backtest scripts in research/team_totals/ use an
entirely different approach (Monte Carlo simulation) that has NO
connection to the formula used in the live signal.
