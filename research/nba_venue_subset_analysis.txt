KEY FINDINGS
================================================================

1. STRONGEST SUBSET: DAL as away team (ME=+9.57, N=41, HR=68.3%)
   WEAKEST SUBSET:  LAL as home team (ME=-0.26, N=43, HR=46.5%)

2. DOMINANT DIMENSION: Individual team identity (spread 9.61 pts)
   The signal is heavily concentrated:
     Top 3 away teams (DAL/UTA/PHI) = 54.3% of total error
     Top 3 home teams (IND/OKC/SAS) = 58.4% of total error
     Bottom home teams (LAL/NOP) effectively zero out the signal

3. LINE BANDS: Signal persists across all bands. No exploitable
   line-level filter. <220 is strongest but low N.

4. ORtg TIERS: Completely flat. Road team quality does NOT drive
   the venue effect. This is a structural interaction, not an
   offense quality story.

5. B2B EFFECT: -3.05 pts penalty when road team is on B2B.
   Rested road warriors generate +7.12 ME vs +4.08 on B2B.

6. SEASON PHASE: Flat. Signal is persistent January through April.
   Slight late-season weakening but still significant (p=0.034).

7. CONCENTRATION RISK: 3 away teams and 3 home venues generate
   most of the signal. BKN (away) and LAL/NOP (home) are dead
   weight. A team-filtered version excluding BKN-away and
   LAL/NOP-home would improve the signal-to-noise ratio.
