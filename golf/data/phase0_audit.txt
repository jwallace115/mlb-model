Q4. DEAD HEAT IN PREDICTIONS?
  NO EFFECT on predictions archive. With/without dead_heat
  parameter, all probability values are IDENTICAL (tested
  with odds_format=percent for Scheffler 2024 Masters:
  win, top_5, top_10, top_20 — all zero difference).
  DG likely handles dead heat internally or the archive
  outputs pre-dead-heat probabilities.

  HOWEVER, dead heat IS in the ODDS DATA:
  bet_outcome_text includes "dead-heat: 1 for 2" and
  "dead-heat: 2 for 3". bet_outcome_numeric gives the
  fractional payout (0.5 for 1-for-2, 0.667 for 2-for-3).
  This means the odds data correctly reflects dead heat
  settlements. Example:
    top_10: Zalatoris finished T10, outcome="dead-heat: 2 for 3"
    top_20: Moore finished T20, outcome="dead-heat: 1 for 2"
