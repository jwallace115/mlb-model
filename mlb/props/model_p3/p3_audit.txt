AUDIT 2 — MODEL PROBABILITY vs IMPLIED PROBABILITY
======================================================================

CRITICAL FINDING:

  HITS implied_prob_over average:  0.5000
  HITS implied_prob_under average: 0.5000
  TB implied_prob_over average:    0.5000
  TB implied_prob_under average:   0.5000

  ALL IMPLIED PROBABILITIES ARE EXACTLY 50.0%.

  This means the devig function is outputting 0.5/0.5 for
  every single play — the best_over_odds and best_under_odds
  fields are NULL or not being passed to the devig function.

  The model's "edge" is therefore:
    edge = model_prob - 0.50

  This is NOT a market comparison. This is the model's
  own probability minus a fixed 50% baseline.

  At 5%+ edge bucket:
    HITS model_prob = 70.7% vs "implied" 50.0% → "edge" = +20.7%
    TB model_prob = 69.5% vs "implied" 50.0% → "edge" = +19.5%
    Actual hit rate: HITS 63.1%, TB 59.3%

  The actual hit rate IS above 50% (genuinely winning), but the
  "edge" calculation is meaningless — it compares model prob to
  a flat 50%, not to the actual market-implied probability.

VERDICT: CRITICAL
  The edge calculation is broken. Implied probabilities default
  to 0.50 because best_over_odds / best_under_odds are missing
  or null in the market_view. The ROI is still genuinely positive
  (actual > 50% hit rate at -110), but the edge buckets are
  based on model_prob distance from 50%, not from market odds.
  The +19.4% ROI headline may still hold, but the edge framework
  is not measuring what it claims to measure.

AUDIT 3 — BEST LINE LOGIC
======================================================================

CONFIRMED:
  - P3 uses consensus_line (median across books) for both OVER
    and UNDER evaluation — same line for both directions. CORRECT.
  - No line shopping between OVER and UNDER.

FINDING:
  best_over_line and best_under_line differ significantly:
  - HITS: mean difference = 1.107 points
  - 66.8% of plays have different best_over vs best_under lines

  Example: Manny Machado best_over=0.5, best_under=2.5, consensus=1.5
  This is a wide market — books disagree significantly on hitter hit lines.

  The ODDS fields (best_over_odds, best_under_odds) appear to be
  NULL in the market_view because the P2 market_view aggregation
  grouped by player and computed consensus/best lines but did NOT
  carry forward per-book odds.

VERDICT: WARNING
  Line logic is correct (consensus used for both directions).
  BUT: odds are missing → devig produces 50/50 → edge calculation
  is broken (see Audit 2). The structural line evaluation is sound
  but the market probability layer is non-functional.

AUDIT 4 — PA PROJECTION ACCURACY
======================================================================

  HITS projected PA: 4.12 vs actual PA: 3.93 (delta: +0.18)
  TB projected PA:   4.11 vs actual PA: 3.92 (delta: +0.20)

  Systematic overestimate: PA is 4.5-5.0% higher than actual.
  Overestimation grows by batting order slot:
    Slot 1: +0.10 PA
    Slot 5: +0.16 PA
    Slot 9: +0.38 PA

  Correlation: 0.31 — weak. PA projection is crude.

  The PA overestimate inflates:
  - Binomial distribution for hits (more trials = higher expected)
  - MC simulation for TB (more PAs = higher expected TB)

  Impact quantification:
  - 0.18 extra PA × 0.245 hit rate = ~0.044 extra projected hits
  - On a 0.5 line, this shifts P(over 0.5) by ~3-5%
  - This directionally explains part of the OVER bias

VERDICT: WARNING
  PA overestimate is systematic (+0.18 PA, ~5%) and worsens
  down the lineup. This inflates model_prob_over and should
  be corrected in P4. However, it does NOT explain the strong
  UNDER signal (UNDER works DESPITE this OVER bias).

======================================================================
NET ASSESSMENT
======================================================================

The positive ROI is REAL — actual hit rates are genuinely above
50% for HITS and TB bets at -110. The model IS finding signal.

However:
1. The "edge %" numbers are meaningless (comparing to 50%, not market)
2. The volume at "5%+ edge" is inflated (93% of plays qualify)
3. PA overestimation inflates OVER predictions
4. The true edge magnitude is unknown until market odds are properly
   integrated

CORRECTIVE ACTIONS REQUIRED BEFORE SHADOW DEPLOYMENT:
1. Fix odds propagation in P2 market_view → carry best_over_odds
   and best_under_odds through to the market view
2. Fix devig to use actual book odds, not default to 50/50
3. Recompute edges against true market-implied probabilities
4. Fix PA projection (use actual historical PA by lineup slot,
   not static lookup table)
5. Re-run P3 backtest with corrected edge framework
6. Only then evaluate whether ROI holds at meaningful edge thresholds
